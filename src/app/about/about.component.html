About

The group develops research on Computer Graphics, Data Visualization, Human-Computer Interaction, Virtual Reality, Image Processing, and Computer Vision. Over the years, the group has contributed to these areas mainly with results from several MSc theses and doctoral dissertations.

The group's former students, in large part, now work in universities and companies in Brazil and abroad. Members of the group regularly serve in program committees of the leading conferences and editorial boards of prestigious journals in these areas and maintain national and international cooperation with researchers from well-known institutions and companies.
